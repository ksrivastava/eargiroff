I am an illustrator, painter and designer who currently lives in Redmond, WA. I have worked as a graphic designer, set designer, cake decorator and freelance artist. I have experience teaching art to elementary students, and leading an art activity at Seattle Art Museum’s Intimate Impressionism Community Day. My passion for art has expanded to include web and UX/UI design. I aim to integrate my illustrative style and of color into my design work to communicate in a simple, beautiful and unique way. You can find my resume here.

Hope you enjoy my portfolio! I’m happy to collaborate on creative projects, teach art lessons and/or do commissioned work. Please contact me if you are interested.
